## Style Prompt

Create a cinematic investment-banking report film for `nwfwa`, an assistive FWA risk and operations platform. Use a deep navy canvas, crisp white typography, disciplined blue accents, thin financial-report rules, sparse tabular structures, and controlled camera-like motion. The mood should feel credible, analytical, and boardroom-ready rather than SaaS-marketing bright.

## Colors

- Background: `#061326` deep navy
- Background secondary: `#0A1F3A` report panel navy
- Foreground: `#F4F8FF` blue-white text
- Muted foreground: `#9EB6D8` secondary labels
- Accent: `#58A6FF` institutional blue
- Signal: `#BFD7FF` pale data highlight

## Typography

- Headline: `Georgia`, `Times New Roman`, serif for report-grade authority
- Body and labels: `Inter`, `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, Arial, sans-serif for operational clarity

## Motion Rules

- Keep motion slow, deliberate, and cinematic: 0.45s to 0.9s entrances.
- Use subtle vertical drift, opacity fades, and scale changes.
- Keep background grid and data lines ambient and low-contrast.
- Avoid energetic bounces, neon glows, or playful transitions.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use purple gradients, neon cyberpunk colors, or generic startup blues.
- Do not make card-heavy SaaS marketing layouts.
- Do not imply automatic claim denial, approval, or fraud accusation.
- Do not use decorative clutter that weakens the investment-report tone.
- Do not hide governance and human-review boundaries.
